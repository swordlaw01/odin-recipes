Pulled Pork

Pulled Pork Sandwich, BBQ sauce, with salad (optional)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground pork
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrot
  • 1/2 cup canned black beans
  • 1/2 cup lettuce
  • 1/2 cup salad
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup crumbled bacon

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add pork and cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove pork from skillet and set aside.
  2. In same skillet, add all other ingredients and cook until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Add pork and serve immediately.
